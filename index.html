Number Guessing Game!

you will be given a random number a the start of the game.
the will be six cars below. By clicking on a car you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you loose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each car is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each car.
***YOU MUST CLICK THE START BUTTON TO RESET TO A NEW ROUND***
